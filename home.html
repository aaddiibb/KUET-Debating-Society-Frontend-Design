Formats We Practice

We train across multiple competitive debate styles so members can compete at any level.

British Parliamentary

BP — The Global Standard

Four teams, eight speakers, one motion. BP is used at WUDC and most major Asian university championships. We hold weekly BP rounds and host dedicated prep camps before the national circuit.

Asian Parliamentary

AP — The Regional Circuit

Three teams, tightly structured roles, and a strong tradition of rebuttal. AP is the dominant format at Bangladeshi inter-university competitions. We run structured AP training every semester.

Public Forum

PF — Policy & Evidence

Two teams argue a real-world policy resolution with research-backed arguments. Public Forum sharpens evidence analysis and cross-examination skills essential for national policy debate competitions.

About The Society

KUET Debating Society has grown from a small campus initiative into one of the most active student debate organizations in Bangladesh. We offer weekly practice, inter-department tournaments, and a structured pathway from beginner to national-level competitor.

Whether you are a first-year student who has never debated before, or a returning member aiming for a national title, KDS provides the coaching, community, and competition opportunities to help you grow.
