Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find what you need above? Here are the questions we hear most often.

Who can join KUET Debating Society?

Any currently enrolled KUET student can join. No prior debate experience is required — only a willingness to learn and participate. We welcome students from all departments and all years.

Is there a membership fee?

Membership is completely free. Some external competitions carry registration fees, but KDS covers or subsidizes these for selected members representing KUET.

How do I get selected for national tournaments?

Selection is based on attendance at weekly sessions, performance in internal tournaments, and evaluation by the Training Coordinator. Any member who commits to practice is eligible to try out.

I have no experience — can I still join?

Absolutely. We run a dedicated Freshers Orientation Tournament each semester specifically for new members. Senior members will guide you through the basics before your first round.

Can I collaborate with KDS for an event or workshop?

Yes. We welcome collaborations with other departments, student bodies, and organizations. Use the contact form above and select “Event Collaboration” as the subject.

How do I become an adjudicator?

Adjudicator training is open to all members with at least one semester of active participation. Contact the Training Coordinator through the form above to express interest.
