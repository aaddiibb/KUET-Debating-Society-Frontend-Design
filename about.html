Club History

KUET Debating Society began as a small student initiative committed to improving public speaking and analytical reasoning on campus. Over the years, it grew into one of the most active student communities at KUET.

From weekly internal rounds to national university competitions, the club has consistently encouraged members to challenge assumptions, defend ideas with evidence, and communicate with clarity and confidence.

Today, the society hosts training camps, adjudication workshops, and debate festivals throughout the year, creating opportunities for both beginners and experienced speakers.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to cultivate principled communicators and critical thinkers through inclusive debate culture, rigorous training, and respectful discourse. We aim to empower KUET students to express ideas with confidence, engage in evidence-based argument, and contribute meaningfully to society.
